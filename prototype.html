App Tutorial

  • Home

    This page is where you can find any news or updates regarding the app as well as a place where you can remind yourself of what each tab is used for!

  • Write

    This is the page of your music write dreams! You have the ability to write out lyrics and chords, as well as melodies and layer them together.

    On top of that, this page can instantly transpose your songs for you. This page also allows you to be able to orginize your songs into seperate singles or albums.

  • Share

    Here is where you can share your music with the world or just your closest friends.

    Once you've grinded out and made that perfect song(s), you can finally share those melodies with anyone you want to!

  • Learn

    On this page, you will be able to sharpen your skills.

    You can learn some foundational music theory and you can ask questions about writing or get inspiration from other songwriters, or you could simply just chat about whatever your heart desires!

    Our goal is to further connect the world with music!
