Simply Writing Music

Simply Writing Music is an app that helps musicians begin writing their own music by teaching them basic music theory, providing them with an orginized software that aids them in their creativity, allows the users to share their music, and give tips to and learn from other song writers, as well as passing down their own experience to other song writers. Whether you're brand new to writing music, or you've been writing your whole life, this app will give you the tools to solidify, orginize, and share your music to the world!

Our Goal

We believe that music brings people together. Our goal is to aid those wishing to share their melodic voice with the world and share their stories with others. We also want to create a place where musicians can easily come together and teach and learn from each other to help grow our musical community.
